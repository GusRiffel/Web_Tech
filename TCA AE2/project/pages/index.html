Coronavirus

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. It is currently affecting 218 countries and territories around the world and 2 international conveyances. Coronaviruses (CoV) are a large family of viruses that cause illness ranging from the common cold to more serious diseases such as Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS-CoV). The 2019 novel coronavirus is a new strain that has not been seen in humans until now and has caused viral pneumonia.
